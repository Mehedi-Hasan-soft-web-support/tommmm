Call for Papers

The International Conference on Intelligent Data Analysis and Applications (IDAA 2025) invites original research papers in areas including Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Big Data, IoT, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Cybersecurity, and Smart Systems. The conference will be held on 5–6 December 2025 at Daffodil International University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Accepted and presented papers will be submitted to IEEE Xplore, provided they meet IEEE standards. Join us to share innovations, foster collaborations, and expand your professional network in the evolving field of intelligent data analysis.

IEEE Publication: All accepted and presented papers will be submitted for inclusion in IEEE Xplore Digital Library. Papers must meet IEEE scope and quality standards. The IDAA Conference Proceedings will be indexed in the prestigious IEEE Xplore Digital Library.
